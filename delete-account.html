Delete Your Account

FaiCora  ·  HriGa

You can delete your FaiCora account and all associated data using either of the methods below.

1Delete directly in the app (recommended)

Open FaiCora → tap your profile icon → scroll down → tap "Delete Account". Your account and all cloud-stored data will be permanently deleted immediately.

2Request deletion by email

Send an email to HriGaBV@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request – FaiCora" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email & credentials)
  • Any cloud-stored analysis data linked to your account
  • Firebase Analytics identifiers associated with your account

What is not deleted

  • Anonymised, aggregated crash logs retained by Firebase Crashlytics (cannot be attributed back to you)
  • Local data stored on your device — uninstalling the app removes this

⚠ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your analysis history will be lost unless you export it first via Settings → Export Backup.
