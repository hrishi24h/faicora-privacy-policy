Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. About Us

FaiCora is developed and operated by HriGa. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at HriGaBV@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use FaiCora:

  • Account information: Your email address and authentication data when you register or sign in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Analysis data: Stock symbols you analyse and the AI-generated reports produced for those analyses. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • API keys: If you enter third-party API keys (e.g. Marketaux), these are stored in encrypted form on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: If the app crashes, anonymised crash reports are automatically sent to Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.
  • Usage analytics: Basic usage events (e.g. screens visited) may be collected through Firebase to help us improve the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain the FaiCora service.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Process AI analysis requests via our Firebase Cloud Functions. Your stock symbol and price data are sent to the function; the OpenAI API key is stored server-side and never on your device.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app stability.
  • Improve the app based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, crash reporting, and cloud functions hosting (Google Privacy Policy).
  • OpenAI — AI-generated analysis reports are produced by OpenAI's API, invoked server-side through our Cloud Functions. Only the stock symbol and financial summary are sent; no personal account data is shared with OpenAI.
  • Yahoo Finance / Marketaux — publicly available market price and news data is fetched from these APIs. No personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Analysis history is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. It is not uploaded to our servers.
  • You can export, delete, or clear your data at any time via Settings → Backup & Restore or Settings → Clear Cache.
  • Firebase Authentication stores your account credentials in accordance with Google's data retention policies.
  • We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • API keys are stored using Android EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256 encryption).
  • All network traffic uses HTTPS — cleartext connections are blocked at the OS level.
  • Firebase security rules restrict access to authenticated users only.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

FaiCora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at HriGaBV@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your analysis history at any time from Settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at HriGaBV@gmail.com.
